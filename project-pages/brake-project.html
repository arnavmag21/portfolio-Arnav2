Brake Pedal and Throttle Base Redesign
I used Siemens-NX to redesign the brake pedal and throttle base for the 2024-2025 Cal Poly Formula SAE car.
Project Details / Background
On the Cal Poly Formula SAE Racing team I act as a lead engineer on the brakes subsection. Using Siemens-NX I redesigned the brake pedal with a focus on performance, ergonomics, and weight optimization. The pedal was reengineered with a curved geometry to improve normal force distribution and better suit the drivers preference.
I redesigned the throttle base, also using Siemens-NX, to remove unnecessary weight while maintaining structural integrity, reducing overall weight to improve vehicle responsiveness and acceleration.
